# Privacy Policy for School Mangement System

**Last Updated: December 7, 2025**

## Introduction

School Mangement System ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the App:

- **Account Information**: Email address, full name, profile picture (optional), and bio (optional)
- **Institution Information**: School/coaching center name, address, contact details
- **Student Data**: Student names, contact information, guardian details, photos, enrollment dates
- **Teacher Data**: Teacher names, contact information, subject assignments
- **Payment Information**: Fee records, payment transactions, receipt details (no credit card information is collected)

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, session duration, crash reports, performance metrics
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, app errors, diagnostic information

### 3. Data from Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

- **Google Sign-In**: For authentication (email, name, profile picture)
- **Firebase Services**: For backend infrastructure, analytics, and crash reporting
- **Cloud Storage**: For storing photos and documents

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### Primary Functions
- **Account Management**: Creating and managing user accounts
- **Data Storage**: Storing student, teacher, batch, and payment information
- **Fee Tracking**: Recording and tracking fee payments and dues
- **Receipt Generation**: Creating payment receipts and reports
- **Analytics**: Understanding app usage and improving user experience
- **Communication**: Sending notifications about payments, updates, and features

### Secondary Functions
- **App Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features
- **Error Detection**: Identifying and fixing bugs and crashes
- **Security**: Preventing fraud and unauthorized access
- **Compliance**: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- **Cloud Storage**: Data is stored on Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage (Google Cloud Platform servers)
- **Local Storage**: Some data is cached locally on your device using SQLite for offline access

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication via Firebase Auth and Google Sign-In
- **Access Control**: Role-based access to data
- **Regular Backups**: Automated backup systems
- **Security Rules**: Firestore security rules to protect user data

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Data**: When you delete data through the app, it is permanently removed from our servers
- **Account Deletion**: Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### We May Share Data With:
- **Service Providers**: Firebase (Google), Cloud Storage providers who help us operate the App

- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
